## 🚀 How to Use ATS Defender

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Upload Your Resume**
   - Click "Browse Files" or drag/drop your resume
   - Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT
   - Maximum size: 5MB

2. **Enter Job Information**
   - Job Title (required)
   - Company Name
   - Complete Job Description (required)
   - Required Skills
   - Select ATS Aggressiveness Level

3. **Run Analysis**
   - Click "Analyze & Optimize for ATS"
   - Watch real-time processing indicators
   - Review detailed compatibility scores

4. **Review Results**
   - Examine 3-score breakdown (Keyword, Format, Overall)
   - See specific improvements made
   - Review optimized resume content

5. **Download Optimized Resume**
   - Click "Download Optimized Resume"
   - Save as TXT file with timestamp
   - Use for job applications

## 📊 Optimization Metrics

### Keyword Match Score
- **Calculation**: Percentage of job keywords found in resume
- **Target**: 85%+ for optimal ATS compatibility
- **Improvement**: Strategic keyword insertion and emphasis

### Format Compatibility Score
- **Factors**: Section headers, bullet points, spacing, structure
- **Target**: 90%+ for maximum ATS friendliness
- **Improvement**: Standardized formatting and organization

### Overall ATS Score
- **Calculation**: Weighted average (60% keywords, 40% format)
- **Target**: 85%+ for high compatibility
- **Levels**: 
  - 🏆 85%+: Highly Compatible
  - ✅ 70-84%: Good Compatibility
  - ⚠️ Below 70%: Needs Improvement
